We do hair styles as you need

Canadian, Executive Contour and Undercut - these men's haircuts have long been classics. Men all over the world appreciate them for their striking appearance with minimal care.

We do these styles and more. They do not require much attention, but they provide a strong image. Would you agree, it is difficult to imagine a man putting curls in his hair for 30 minutes. The maximum that we are prepared to do is apply a drop of gel and a few hand movements.

The Barbershop is exclusively for men, so we know about this and many other male facts. Knowledge and experience allow us to maintain a high level of quality and customers to be confident.

We do shaving with a straight razor

A hot towel, shaving cream, and the sensation of a razor moving smoothly across their face - that was what our grandfathers experienced when they visited the barber. With us, at The Barbershop, you can share their experience.

Believe me, this is not the same as scratching yourself with a common razor in front of a steamy mirror. Especially if you add great music and an authentic barber's chair.

Even if you have a beard a razor has a purpose. We can groom your beard with smooth edging and careful shaping to accentuate a man's facial charateristics.

We also take care of your hands

What is a man's manicure? In our understanding, it is basic hand care. It is a subtle thing, but it maximises the effect of beautiful watches and elegant shirts.

Cut burrs, make nails and cuticles tidy... It sounds simple, but it requires great skill to do it well. That is why our team has a manicure master with more than 15 years of experience.

People perceive a man not only by his appearance but also by his handshake. After all, the first thing we do when we meet a friend, colleague, or partner is stretching out our hand to him. We want to enable you to do it more boldly... Therefore, we invite you to The Barbershop not only for a haircut but also for well-groomed hands
